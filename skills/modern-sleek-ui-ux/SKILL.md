# Modern Sleek UI/UX (The Premium Standard)

> **"Precision over decoration."**
> **Philosophy:** Minimalist, high-contrast, and insanely snappy.

---

## 🎨 The "Sleek" Checklist
- [ ] **Dark Mode First**: Use deep grays (`#09090b`) instead of pure black.
- [ ] **Subtle Borders**: Use `border-white/10` or `border-zinc-800` for depth.
- [ ] **Typography**: Inter or Geist—tight letter spacing (-0.02em).
- [ ] **Glassmorphism**: Subtle `backdrop-blur-md` on navigation and overlays.
- [ ] **Micro-shadows**: Use extremely small, sharp shadows for elevation.

---

## ✅ Best Practices
- ✅ **Contrast**: High contrast for text, low contrast for layout lines.
- ✅ **Spacing**: 8px (base-4) grid strictly enforced.
- ✅ **Interactive feedback**: Every button should have a `whileTap` or `whileHover` (Framer).
- ✅ **Empty States**: Minimalist illustrations or clean typography.

---

## ❌ Anti-Patterns
- ❌ **Bright Primary Colors**: Avoid "neon" unless for specific accents.
- ❌ **Rounded Corners (>12px)**: Keep it professional with `rounded-md` (6px) or `rounded-lg` (8px).
- ❌ **Large Shadows**: Avoid soft, "blobby" shadows; they look dated.
